Project Ethics & Considerations

Understanding the scope, limitations, and future improvements of our Data Employment Skills Dashboard.

Data Source & Licensing

  • Data sourced from Kaggle (2024 LinkedIn Data Science Job Listings dataset).
  • Licensed under the Open Data Commons Attribution License (ODC-By) v1.0, allowing free use with proper attribution.
  • Only includes job postings from the United States due to data availability constraints.

Scope & Limitations

  • Currently focuses on 14 Data Science job categorizations with at least 50-100 job listings.
  • Data cleaning and standardization efforts were performed to improve usability.
  • Due to time constraints, some job postings and niche roles may not be fully represented.

Technical Considerations

  • Data is stored in a MongoDB database for efficient querying.
  • The backend uses Flask API to serve job skill recommendations.
  • The frontend dashboard is built using JavaScript for interactive filtering & ranking.

Future Enhancements

  • Exploring live job APIs & web scraping to track real-time job market trends.
  • Implementing custom search filters to refine results by seniority level & specialization.
  • Expanding job categorization to include **global** listings where data quality permits.
